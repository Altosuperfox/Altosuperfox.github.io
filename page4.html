GETTING INVOLVED IN THE FURRY FANDOM

First, I would recommend getting furry social media. This can be from Facebook to twitter furry amino and google+ these apps/websites have either groups or communities that circle around the furry fandom. Furry amino don’t get me started this app goes on your phone (sadly it does not work on desktop) it is all about furry it has a featured page where if your lucky some of your posts might show up there. There is chat where you can chat with other furies and much more. Google+ has two groups that I suggest you should join “★║FURRIES║★” and “Furry Fandom” both have a substantial amount of furies in them and their will always be something new.
